Keep podcasting open and free

As podcasting has grown in popularity, larger organizations are looking to find new ways to monitize podcasts at the cost of an open ecosystem. "Exclusive podcasts" are not podcasts at all.

The Open Ecosystem

Podcasts are a miracle of the open web. Here's why.

Any individual or organization can record and publish a podcast to the web. There's no gatekeeper.

Podcasts can be found on popular indexes or added via a URL.

Pick your favorite podcast client just like you do for email.

Podcasting is unlike any media

Podcasting is decentralized and based on open web-standards. This is it's greatest strength. Similar to email, no one entity controls podcasting. So anyone can start a podcast with just some basic equipment and a bit of hosting.
